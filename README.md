## BRIEF DESCRIPTION
- The Shift-Key Car Rental Database is a centralized relational hub designed to manage the full lifecycle of a rental fleet, from the moment a customer hits "book" to the final maintenance check after a return. By housing interconnected data on vehicles, branch locations, and customer profiles, the system acts as a high-integrity "single source of truth" that prevents logistical nightmares like double-bookings and automates complex financial calculations like late fees and insurance premiums. It essentially transforms messy fleet logistics into a streamlined digital engine, allowing managers to track vehicle health and revenue trends while giving frontline staff the real-time accuracy they need to keep the wheels turning, minus the paperwork-induced headaches.
